Fortnite FPS Mode Announced

Fortnite is breaking new ground by introducing a First-Person Shooter (FPS) mode, a highly anticipated feature that’s expected to change the way players experience the game. Known for its third-person battle royale mode, Fortnite will now offer fans a fresh take on the action.

Epic Games revealed the mode in a recent update, giving players a sneak peek at how the game will look and feel from a first-person perspective.

With the new FPS mode, players can expect tighter combat mechanics and a closer, more immersive experience as they drop into their favorite locations. This update could bring even more strategic depth to Fortnite’s already popular gameplay.
