Gboard’s Unusual Möbius Strip Keyboard

The latest creation from Gboard's Japanese department has people scratching their heads—a keyboard shaped like a Möbius strip. This peculiar design has caused quite a stir, with many wondering if it’s even real or simply a joke.

The trailer for the keyboard humorously addresses the frustration of using a keyboard upside down, which led many to believe it might be an elaborate prank. However, the keyboard design is indeed real, and while it won’t be produced commercially, a 3D model of the keyboard has been released on GitHub for the curious to explore.

Although this isn’t something that will end up on your desk, the Möbius strip keyboard serves as a reminder of the creativity and playfulness that Gboard brings to their designs. Who knows what they might come up with next?
