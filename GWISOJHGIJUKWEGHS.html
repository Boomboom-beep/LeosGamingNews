Gaming News

Microsoft Acquires Activision Blizzard

The UK's Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) has provisionally cleared Microsoft's acquisition of Activision Blizzard, a significant step towards the deal's completion. This move would solidify Microsoft's position as the world's largest gaming company, raising concerns about potential market dominance.

FIFA's Interactive World Cup in Disarray

After a bitter split with EA Sports, FIFA is struggling to secure a partner for its interactive World Cup tournament. This leaves the future of the tournament in doubt and raises questions about the potential impact on the gaming community.

Next Xbox Revealed in Secret Documents (Allegedly)

Court documents have reportedly revealed details about Microsoft's next Xbox console. This comes as a surprise, as the company had not officially announced any new hardware. Fans are eager to learn more about the console's features and release date.
